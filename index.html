About Me

Ixtlan T. Banawa is an enthusiastic student hailing from the renowned Herman Harrell Horne School. With an insatiable curiosity and an unwavering passion for learning, he is a shining example of the power of dedication and determination.

His academic journey is marked by a fervor for his favorite subjects: Mathematics, Science, and English. These disciplines not only captivate his attention but also fuel his desire to explore and understand the world around him.

Beyond his academic pursuits, Ixtlan possesses a unique creative flair as a Blender 3D modeler. His adeptness in the world of 3D design has led him to craft a multitude of captivating 3D assets, which find their home in both the realms of gaming and stunning visual renders. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for innovation, Ixtlan brings his creations to life through the power of Blender.

His toolkit extends further as he showcases his expertise in Photoshop, allowing him to manipulate and enhance images with a masterful touch. His digital prowess knows no bounds as he skillfully transforms concepts into vibrant visual realities.

In his leisure moments, Ixtlan finds solace in the digital world of YouTube. A true connoisseur of content, he enjoys immersing himself in a     vari       variety of videos that cater to his diverse interests.

               With an insuppressible thirst for knowledge and an impressive range of skills, Ixtlan continues to illuminate the path of learning,                              creativity, and discovery. His journey stands as a testament to the unbreakable spirit of a student who embraces the present                             while shaping a dynamic future.
